How to join the whitelist¶

  • Is there a friend told you about Beyou Network, Stoken – Unique token in the Beyou Network ecosystem, and Stoken Public-Sale? Congratulations!! Ask him for a whitelisted address.

  • Use your ETH wallet, send 1,001 Stoken to the address which is whitelisted already.

  • You will receive 1 Stoken back, as a success signal.

  • Finished. Your wallet address has already whitelisted.

Note

Set gas limit to 600,000 or more, the rest will be returned automatically.

Related link: How to set gas limit and gas price?
