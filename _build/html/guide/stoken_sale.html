How to participate in Stoken Public-Sale?¶

If you are fresh for Stoken, at first, you need a ETH wallet, and follow the 2 steps below:

  1. Join the whitelist

  2. Participate in Stoken Public-Sale

If you are interest in earning ETH and Stoken, please read Stoken Referral Program.

1. Join the whitelist¶

Full version: How to join the whitelist

Send 1,001 Stoken to an address which is whitelisted already, you will receive 1 Stoken back, that is a success signal for signed-up.

Note

Set gas limit to 600,000 or more, the rest will be returned automatically.

Related link: How to set gas limit and gas price?

Then, you will enjoy a gift of BUY ONE AND GET ONE MORE FREE during the Stoken Public-Sale.

And, if you share your whitelisted address to a friend, he will get the gift too, and you can earn ETH and Stoken, according to Stoken Referral Program.

2. Participate in in Stoken public-sale¶

Full version: How to join the whitelist

Send ETH to the address of stoken2_sale_contract, you will get Stoken automatically, with a gift of BUY ONE AND GET ONE MORE FREE.

0xd2F14958777BA77f118F5Adb0A3d6C8688909786

  1. Use your own ETH Wallet, choose Send ETH.

  2. Enter the address above, or scan the qr-code.

  3. Set Gas Limit >= 6,000,000, upper limit is 8,000,000. If the transaction interrupted with out of gas, all the unused ETH will return automatically.

  4. Set Gas Price, if you want transaction speed up, you could choose higher price, or leave it as default.

  5. Send the transaction.

  6. Waiting for Ethereum blockchain to confirm.

  7. Receive Stoken.

Note

Make sure you have set gas limit to 6,000,000 or more, the rest will be returned automatically.

Related link: How to set gas limit and gas price?
