How to shorten the reserved time of giftÂ¶

The gift for whitelist will be reserved in 6 months (180 days).

Both making direct sales (Lv.1) and increasing your holding will take effect:

We assume that the gift amount (of reserved Stoken) is m Stoken.

  • Making direct sale (Lv.1) for every m Stoken, reduced by 1 day.
    Reduced to at least 4 months (120 days).
  • Increasing holding for every m Stoken, reduced by 1 months (30 days).
    Reduced to at least 3 months (90 days).
